About Seoyon E-Hwa Savannah

Seoyon E-Hwa Manufacturing Savannah, Inc. aims to establish a cutting-edge manufacturing facility at Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center, Georgia. Specializing in eco-friendly, high-performance components for electric vehicles (EVs), the facility will utilize advanced technologies, specialized equipment, and new materials. With established partnerships with renowned automotive manufacturers, Seoyon E-Hwa aligns with the growing demand for EVs and contributes to advanced energy technologies, fostering sustainable transportation solutions.
